## 8. RISK MITIGATION

### Risk: Low signup rate
- **Mitigation:** A/B test messaging, try multiple channels, adjust pricing
- **Fallback:** Focus on API tier (higher-value customers)

### Risk: High churn rate
- **Mitigation:** Email onboarding sequence, weekly tips, community building
- **Fallback:** Offer annual plans (lock in revenue)

### Risk: Payment processing issues
- **Mitigation:** Test Stripe integration thoroughly before launch
- **Fallback:** Manual payment option (Stripe invoice)

### Risk: Model accuracy drops
- **Mitigation:** Continuous retraining, monitor performance metrics
- **Fallback:** Transparency (show actual vs. predicted results)
